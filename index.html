The Heritage Quest

Discover the real stories of Mille Fleurs

You are standing inside a piece of Trinidad and Tobago's history. The Heritage Quest is a guided journey through the grounds of Mille Fleurs.

Pick up your Quest Passport at the registration table in the Games Yard. Follow the clues to our three Marshal Stops. Learn about the people who shaped this house, get your passport stamped, and uncover parts of this yard that most people pass their whole lives without ever entering.

Sign up closes at 3:30 PM. The Quest is free and open to all families.

The Optometrist Today AR Egg Hunt

Trinidad was once part of a vast Ice Age continent. Today, the animals that roamed that world have returned to Mille Fleurs.

1 Search the grounds for hidden Easter eggs.
2 Open an egg to find a QR code and a secret code word.
3 Enter the code word below to unlock the animal.
4 Tap the button to see the animal appear in front of you. Please leave the egg exactly where you found it.
